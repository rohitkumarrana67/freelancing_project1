Cogent ERP

Cogent ERP is a Complete Enterprise Resource Planning Solutions for Education, Healthcare, Hospitality, Distribution, Operations and Processing and more. We have a unique proposition in offering ERP solutions and implementation methodology, with a specific objective to reduce the total cost of ownership (TCO) and to make the solution completely tailor made for the client. As a matter of fact, while most of the standard ERP systems in the market support the Finance & Distribution functions that are normally generic in nature, they fail to fully support the core vertical process of organizations. We are addressing this problem by offering a total ERP solution that is a blend of Merp standard ERP package (Finance, Distribution and HR & Payroll) with a fully customized industry vertical for the customer.

✪ Multi company structure

✪ Multi currency accounting

✪ Supports all standard functionalities in Accounting, Distribution, HR and payroll processes
